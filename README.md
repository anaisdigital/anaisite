# anaisite
Who am I?

My name is Anaïs, and if you are reading this in 2226, I hope you've finally reached the Post-Scarcity world I dreamed of. I am a trans woman, lesbian, atheist, and neurodivergent (Austistic/ADHD/AHSD). In 2026, I am a "rebel without a voice," surviving in a hostile environment, fighting for my financial autonomy, and hiding from a society (and a family) that refuses to see me for who I really am.

# What is this Repository?
It is a digital footprint of my existence. While the world of 2026 is driven by greed and "money-first" history, I chose to build things based on humanism, nature, and emotion.

# Dear Future:
I lived through a time where living was "shit" because greed was the priority. I spent my days building LEGO MOCs, simulating space flights in KSP, and coding in HTML/CSS/JS to create worlds where I could finally breathe.

If you found this, I survived. My ideas survived. The Zenith survived.
